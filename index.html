QUICK TIPS

Start Strong

01

Keep knees bent

Soft knees absorb the wake's impact and give you balance when the water gets choppy.

02

Let the boat do the work

Don't pull the rope — maintain consistent edge pressure and let the boat's momentum carry you up.

03

Eyes on the horizon

Where your head goes, your body follows. Look forward, not down at the board.

04

Commit to the edge

Half-hearted edge cuts cause crashes. Load the line with confidence and hold through the wake.
